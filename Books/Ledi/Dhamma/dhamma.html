Venerable Ledi Sayādaw

Dhamma Dīpanī: A Manual of the Dhamma

Translated by U Han Htay

Edited by Bhikkhu Pesala

    This book comprises the Ledi Sayādaw’s response to thirteen questions posed by devout laymen who were concerned with the improper behaviour of certain monks. All monks, and everyone who has dealings with monks, should read it carefully to know how to make merit and avoid demerit.
