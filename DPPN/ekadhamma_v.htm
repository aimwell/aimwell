• Ekadhamma Vagga

1. Ekadhamma Vagga.-The sixteenth chapter of the Eka-nipāta of the Anguttara Nikāya. It contains ten suttas, in which ten subjects of reflection (anussati) are mentioned as being conducive to inner emancipation. A.i.30.

2. Ekadhamma Vagga.-The first chapter of the Anāpānā Samyutta of the Samyutta Nikaya. S.v.311-41.
