• Dutiya Sutta

1. Dutiya Sutta.-On the four modes of progress: painful with sluggish intuition and with swift intuition, and pleasant, with the same two varieties of intuition. A.ii.154.

2. Dutiya Sutta.-Questions asked by a deva and the Buddha’s reply thereto. “What has a good man as his companion?” is one question, and the reply thereto is “faith.” (S.i.38).
