• Sārajja Sutta

1. Sārajja Sutta. Five qualities which give confidence to a monk: faith, virtue, learning, energy, insight. A.iii.127.

2. Sārajja Sutta. The same as Sutta (1). A.iii.183.

3. Sārajja Sutta. Abstention from the five sins (taking life, theft, etc.) gives a monk confidence. A.iii.203.
