• Lokavipatti Sutta

1. Lokavipatti Sutta. On eight worldly conditions which obsess the world and round which it revolves: gain and loss, fame and obscurity, blame and praise, contentment and pain. A.iv.156.

2. Lokavipatti Sutta. Average people are obsessed by the eight worldly conditions mentioned in Sutta 1; not so the Ariyan disciple who knows their impermanence. A.iv.157.
