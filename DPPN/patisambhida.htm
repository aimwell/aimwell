• Patisambhidā Sutta

1. Patisambhidā Sutta

Five qualities which make a monk become that which he should: the four patisambhidā and knowledge of all things, great and small, which must be done for his fellow celibates. A.iii.113.

2. Patisambhidā Sutta

Seven qualities which enable a monk to realize, unaided, the four patisambhidā. A.iv.32f.
