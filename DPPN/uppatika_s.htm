• Uppatika Sutta

On the five controlling faculties (indriyāni) - of discomfort, of unhappiness, of ease, of happiness and of indifference — and on how they are conditioned and how they cease to be (S.v.213f). According to the Commentary (SA.iii.192), the title of this sutta should be Uppatipātika Sutta (the sutta which deals with reference to what has gone before).
