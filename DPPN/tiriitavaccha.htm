• Tirītavaccha

1. Tirītavaccha.-The Bodhisatta born as a brahmin in Kāsi. See the Titītavaccha Jātaka.

2. Tirītavaccha.-A setthi of Aritthapura, father of Ummadantī (J.v.210, 211). He is also called Tirīti°. J.v.215; ThigA.i.192.

3. Tirītavaccha.-A brahmin, purohita of Candappajjota and father of Mahā Kaccāna. His wife was Candapadūmā. v.l. Tidivavaccha. Ap.ii.465; ThagA.i.485.
