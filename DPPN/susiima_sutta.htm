• Susīma Sutta

1. Susīma Sutta. Describes the visit of Susīmadevaputta (Susīma 4) to the Buddha. S.i.63f.

2. Susīma Sutta. Gives Sakka’s admonition to his son, Susīma (5). S.i.217.

3. Susīma Sutta. Describes the visit of Susīma Paribbājaka to the Buddha, and their conversation. See Susīma (6). S.ii.119ff.
