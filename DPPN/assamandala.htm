• Assamandala

1. Assamandala.-A ford on the Mahāvālukagangā in Ceylon (Cv.lxxii.27). Geiger refers to a legend which connects this with Kacchakatittha, in which case it should be near the Mahāgantota, east of Polounaruva. Cv.Trs.ii.321, n.5.

2. Assamandala.-One of the spots included in the area marked off by Devānampiyatissa for the Sīmā of the Mahāvihāra. Mv.xv.15 in Appendix B to Geiger’s Edition.

3. Assamandala.-A village near Hallolagāma. Ras.ii.121.
