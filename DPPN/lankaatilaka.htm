• Lankātilaka

1. Lankātilaka. An image house in Pulatthipura, built by Parakkamabāhu I. The standing image of the Buddha (also built by the king), which it contained, bore the name of Lankātilaka too. Cv.lxxviii.53 L, 63; for identification see Cv.Trs.ii.108, n.2,4.

2. Lankātilaka. A park in Ceylon, laid out by Parakkamabāhu I. Cv.lxxix.9.

3. Lankātilaka. A monastery near the modern Kandy, restored by Parakkamabāhu VI. (Cv.xci.30). It was built by Bhuvanekabāhu IV.  Codrington: op. cit., 83.
