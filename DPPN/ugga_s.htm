• Ugga Sutta

1. Ugga Sutta.-Records the conversation between the Buddha and Ugga (2) (q.v.), minister of King Pasenadi. A.iv.6-7.

2. Ugga Sutta.-Deals with the eight wonderful qualities of Ugga of Vesalī. See Ugga (5).

A.iv.208-12.

3. Ugga Sutta.-Deals with the eight wonderful qualities of Ugga of Hatthigāmaka. See Ugga (4). A.iv.212-6.
