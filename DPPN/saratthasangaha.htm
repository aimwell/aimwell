• Sāratthasangaha

1. Sāratthasangaha. A religious work, ascribed to Buddhappiya. Gv. 60, 71; P.L.C. 222.

2. Sāratthasangaha. A religious work, in sixty sections, by Siddhattha Thera, written in the thirteenth century A.C. It deals with various topics of religious interest. P.L.C. 229f.

3. Sāratthasangaha. A religious work (Sārārthasangraha), written in Singhalese, by Saranankara Sangharāja. Cv.xcvii.57.
