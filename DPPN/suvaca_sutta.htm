• Suvaca Sutta

1. Suvaca Sutta. A deva tells the Buddha of seven things which lead to a monk’s growth:

  1. reverence for the Buddha,
  2. the Dhamma,
  3. the Sangha,
  4. the training,
  5. concentration,
  6. fair speech,
  7. good friendship. A.iv.29.

2. Suvaca Sutta.  Sāriputta explains the foregoing sutta. A.iv.30.
