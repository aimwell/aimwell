• Sāradassī

1. Sāradassī Thera. He lived in Nayyinyua in Ava, in the seventeenth century. He was the author of the Gūlhatthadīpanī on the Abhidhamma and of the Visuddhimaggaganthipada. He translated the Nettippakarana into Burmese. He was blamed for indulging in certain luxuries, such as a head covering and a fan, but he later renounced them and lived in the forest. Sās.116; Bode, op. cit., 56.

2. Sāradassī. A monk of Pagan of the eighteenth century. He wrote the Dhātukathāyojanā. Bode, op. cit., 67.
