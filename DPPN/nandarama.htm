• Nandārāma

1. Nandārāma

The pleasances in which Dīpankara Buddha preached his first sermon. It was also the scene of his death. v.l. Sunandārāma. Bu.ii.212, 220.

2. Nāndārāma

The place where Padumuttara Buddha died. Bu.xi.31.

3. Nandārāma

A pleasance in Sunandavatī where Tissa Buddha died (Bu.xviii.28; BuA.192). v.1. Sunandārāma.
