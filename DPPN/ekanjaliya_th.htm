• Ekañjaliya Thera

1. Ekañjaliya Thera.-An arahant. Ninety-two kappas ago he provided a place of residence for the Buddha Tissa in an udumbara-grove and spread for him a carpet of flowers and paid homage to him with clasped hands. Once he was a king, called Ekañjalika. Ap.i.236.

2. Ekañjalika Thera.-An arahant. Ninety-four kappas ago he saw the Pacceka Buddha Romasa, on the banks of a river and, being pleased with his radiant appearance, paid homage to him with clasped hands. Ap.i.281.
