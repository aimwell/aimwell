• Niddasavatthu

1. Niddasavatthu Sutta

The seven constituent qualities of a niddasa (khīnāsava). A.iv.15.

2. Niddasavatthu Sutta

The same preached in answer to a question by Sāriputta as to whether it is right to say that one who has observed celibacy for twelve years could be called a niddasa. A.iv.34ff.

3. Niddasavatthu Sutta

Similar to No. 2, but the questioner is Ānanda and the qualities are differently stated. A.iv.37f.
