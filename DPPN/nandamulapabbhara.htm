• Nandamūlapabbhāra

A mountain cave in Gandhamādana. It is the residence of Pacceka Buddhas and has three caves:

  • Suvannaguhā,
  • Maniguhā and
  • Rajataguhā.

In front of the Maniguhā stands the Mañjū-saka tree (SNA.i.86; DhA.i.226; J.ii.195; etc.).

The mountain is in the northern Himālayas. J.iv.367
