• Kalingara Sutta

Preached at the Kūtāgārasālā in Vesālī. As long as the Licchavis sleep on straw (kalingara) so long will they be able to protect themselves from their enemy, Ajātasattu; when they begin to sleep on soft couches they will suffer defeat. Thus it is also with monks and Māra. S.ii.267f.
