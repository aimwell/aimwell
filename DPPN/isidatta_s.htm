• Isidatta Sutta

1. Isidatta Sutta.-Records the questions of Citta-gahapati and the answers given by Isidatta Thera on the diversity of the elements. S.iv.283-5.

2. Isidatta Sutta.-The same, on the various views that arise in the world. Isidatta answers that they are all due to sakkāyaditthi; he then proceeds, in reply to further questions, to explain how sakkāyaditthi arises and how its absence is brought about. S.iv.286-8.
