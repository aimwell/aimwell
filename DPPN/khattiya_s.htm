• Khattiya Sutta

1. Khattiya Sutta.-A conversation between a devatā and the Buddha regarding the four best things of the world — best of bipeds, quadrupeds, of wives, and of sons. S.i.6.

2. Khattiya Sutta.-A conversation between Jīnussonī and the Buddha on the ends of their efforts as envisaged by khattiyas, brahmanas, house-holders, women, thieves and recluses. A.iii.362f.
