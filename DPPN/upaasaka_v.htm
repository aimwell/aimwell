• Upāsaka Vagga

1. Upāsaka Vagga.-The second chapter of the Brāhmana Samyutta. S.i.172ff.

2. Upāsaka Vagga.-The eighteenth chapter of the Pañcaka Nipāta of the Anguttara Nikāya; on various matters, chiefly connected with laymen. A.iii.203-18.

3. Upāsaka Vagga.-The tenth chapter of the Dasaka Nipāta of the Anguttara Nikāya. A.v.176-206.
