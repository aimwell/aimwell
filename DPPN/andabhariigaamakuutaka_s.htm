• Andabharīgāmakūtaka Sutta

Story of a village cheat, born as a peta.

His secret organs (anda) were huge in size.

He was among the petas seen by Mahāmoggallāna on his way to Rājagaha from Gijjhakūta, in company with the Elder Lakkhana.

He had been a corrupt judge in Rājagaha and had taken bribes and given unjust judgments. S.ii.258, SA.ii.162.
