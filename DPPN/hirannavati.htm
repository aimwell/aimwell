• Hiraññavatī

1. Hiraññavatī. Another name for Bhagalavatī, the residence of Kuvera. J.vi.269, 270

2. Hiraññavatī. A river, on the bank of which was the Upavattana Sālavana of the Mallas of  Kusināra, where the Buddha died (D.ii.137).

It is identified with the Little Gandak; it flows through the district of Gorakhpur, about eight miles west of Great Gandak, and falls into the Gogrā, (Sarayū). Law, Geography, 37.
