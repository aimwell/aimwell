2. Sukha Vagga. The seventh chapter of the Eka Nipāta of the Anguttara Nikāya. A.i.80f.

1. Sukha Sutta. A monk who delights in Dhamma, in growth, in renunciation, in solitude, in being free of ill will, and in non diffuseness — such a one lives in happiness and contentment and will entirely destroy the āsavas. A.iii.431.

2. Sukha Sutta. Two discussions between Sāriputta and the Paribbājaka Sāmandakāni at Nālakagāmaka. A.v.120.
