• Pañcaparivenamūla

A monastery in Ceylon.

At one time Abhaya was head of the monks there and interceded with the chief of the Kuthārasabhā in settling the differences between Kittisirimegha and the prince (who afterwards became Parakkamabāhu I.) (Cv.lxvii.61).

A Pañcaparivenādhipati was author of the Pāli medical work, the Bhesajjamañjūsā, in the reign of Parakkamabāhu IV. of Jambuddoni. P.L.C.244.
