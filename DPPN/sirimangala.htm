• Sirimangala

1. Sirimangala. A Burmese monk of the fourteenth century, author of several commentaries on Buddhaghosa’s works (Bode, op. cit., 27). v.l. Sirisumangala.

2. Sirimangala. A monk of Laos of the sixteenth century; he wrote the Mangaladīpanī and a tīkā on the Sankhyāpakāsaka. Bode, op. cit., 47.
