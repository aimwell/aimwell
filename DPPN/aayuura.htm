• Āyūra

Minister of Maddava, king of Benares. When Maddava was grieved at the loss of his wife, Āyūra and his colleague Pukkusa helped the king’s counsellor Senaka to quench the king’s sorrow.

The story is told in the Dasannaka Jātaka (J.iii.337ff).

In the present age Āyūra became Moggallāna (J.iii.341).
