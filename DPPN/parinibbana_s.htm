• Parinibbāna Sutta

1. Parinibbāna Sutta

An account of the death of the Buddha at Kusinārā. It contains the last words of the Buddha and stanzas of Sahampati, Sakka and Anuruddha, uttered immediately after his death. S.i.157; cp. D.ii.156f.; on their difference, see KS.i.196, n.1.

2. Parinibbāna Sutta

On the complete passing away. A.iv.254.
