• Ārammana Sutta

1. Ārammana Sutta.-Some who practise meditation are skilled in concentration, but not in the object of concentration (ārammana), some vice versa, some are skilled in both, some in neither. S.iii.266.

2. Ārammana Sutta.-Some are skilled in the object of concentration but not in the range of it, etc. (As before.) S.iii.275.
