• Kāla Sutta

1. Kāla Sutta.-There are four seasons: one for hearing the Dhamma, one for discussing it, one for attaining calm, and the last for attaining insight. A.ii.140.

2. Kāla Sutta.-The above explained with an illustration. A.ii.140.

3. Kāla Sutta.-There are five gifts which, if given, are seasonable: gifts to a guest, a traveller, a sick person, one in scarcity, and first fruits to a holy man. A.iii.41.
