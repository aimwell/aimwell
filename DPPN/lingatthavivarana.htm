• Lingatthavivarana

A Pāli grammatical work by Subhūtacandana, a monk of Pagan.

Two Commentaries on it were written,

  • the Lingatthavivaranappakāsaka by Ñānasāgara and
  • the Lingatthavivaranatīkā by Uttama.

A work called Lingatthavivaranavinicchaya, by an unknown author, is also mentioned. Gv. 63, 65, 67, 72, 73, 75; Bode, op. cit., 22, n.1.
