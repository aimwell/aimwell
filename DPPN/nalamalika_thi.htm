• Nalamālika Therī

An arahant. Ninety four kappas ago she was a kinnarā on the banks of the Candabhāgā, and, having seen the Buddha, offered him a garland of flowers.

She was queen of the devas thirty six times and queen among men in ten lives (Ap.ii.528f).

She is probably identical with Cittā Therī (ThigA.33f).
