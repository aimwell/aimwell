• Vivāda Sutta

1. Vivāda Sutta. On the six roots of contention: scorn, hypocrisy, envy, deceit, evil mindedness, prejudice. A.iii.334f.; cf. D.iii.246; M.ii.245; Vin.ii.89.

2. Vivāda Suttā. Two suttas, preached in answer to a question by Upāli, on ten things which are the causes of contention. A.v.77f.
