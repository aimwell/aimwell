• Anurārāma

A monastery to the north of Mahāgāma in South Ceylon, built by King Vasabha, who also bestowed on it one thousand karīsa of land in the village of Heligāma (Mhv.xxxv.83; MT.652).

In Vohāratissa’s time the Thera Mahātissa lived there (Mhv.xxxvi.30). King Vasabha also built an uposatha hall for the vihāra (Mhv.xxxvi.37).
