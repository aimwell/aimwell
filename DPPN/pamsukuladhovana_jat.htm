• Pamsukūladhovana Jātaka

The Sumangala Vilāsinī (DA.i.130) mentions a Jātaka by this name, together with the Vessantara Jātaka, and says that the earth trembled at the preaching of these Jātakas.

Fausboll’s edition contains no Jātaka of this name, nor have I been able to trace it elsewhere. It may have some connection with the Pamsukūladhovana pātihāriya, which formed one of the fifteen hundred miracles that assisted the conversion of Uruvela Kassapa. See Vin.i.29.
