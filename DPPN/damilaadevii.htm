• Damilādevī

1. Damilādevī.-Queen consort of Candamukhasiva. She gave her revenue from Manikāragāma to the Issarasamana-vihāra. Mhv.xxxv.48.

2. Damilādevī.-Queen of Mahādāthika-Mahānāga. She was young and beautiful and when she visited Ambatthala, an old monk named Citta fell in love with her and refused to be consoled even though she died soon after. AA.i.13.
