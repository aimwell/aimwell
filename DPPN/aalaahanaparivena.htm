• Ālāhanaparivena

One of the religious buildings constructed in Pulatthipura by Parakkamabāhu I.

Attached to it was a splendid pāsāda for the thera Sāriputta (Cv.lxxviii.48-9).

Geiger (Cv.Trs.ii.107, n.2) identifies this with the group of buildings lying outside the city, now popularly, but wrongly, called the Jetavanārāma.
