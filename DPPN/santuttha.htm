• Santuttha

1. Santuttha. A disciple of the Buddha at Ñātikā. He was born after death in Akanitthābhavana, there to pass entirely away. D.ii.92; S.v.358f.

2. Santuttha. A palace of Konāgamana Buddha in his last lay life. Bu.xxiv.18.

Santuttha Sutta. The Buddha speaks in praise of Mahā Kassapa, his contentment with whatever he receives in the way of robes, alms, lodgings and medicaments. He is an example worthy of imitation. S.ii.194.
