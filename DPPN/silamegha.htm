• Silāmegha

1. Silāmegha. A nunnery (MT.117; Cv.xlviii.139), restored by the queen of Udaya I. Cv.xlix.25.

2. Silāmegha.  A surname of Aggabodhi VI. (Cv.xlviii. 42, 76, 90) and of Sena I. (Cv.l.43).

3. Silāmegha. A Damila chief, also called Silāmeghara; he was an ally of Kulasekhara. Cv.lxxvi. 98, 238, etc.
