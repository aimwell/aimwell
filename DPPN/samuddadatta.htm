• Samuddadatta

1. Samuddadatta. A monk whom Devadatta persuaded to join him in trying to bring about schism in the Order (Vin.ii.196; iii.171). He was a favourite of Thullanandā. Vin.iv. 66.

2. Samuddadatta. A king who traced his descent from Mahāsammata. He was the first of a dynasty of twenty five kings who ruled in Rājagaha. MT.129.
