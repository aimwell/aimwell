• Dhanuggaha Sutta

A man, who can simultaneously seize the arrows shot by four expert bowmen from the four quarters, is possessed of great speed. Far speedier than he are the sun and the moon, but the change taking place in all things surpasses all these in speed (S.i.265).

This seems also to have been called the Dalhadhamma Sutta. E. g., at J.iv.211.
