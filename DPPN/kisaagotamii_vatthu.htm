• Kisāgotamī Vatthu

1. Kisāgotamī Vatthu.-See Kisāgotamī (2). DhA.i.70f

2. Kisāgotamī Vatthu.-The story of Kisāgotamī (1). The introductory part gives an account of her marriage. DhA.ii.270ff

3. Kisāgotamī Vatthu.-The story of Kisāgotamī (1) becoming a sotāpanna. DhA.iii.432f.

4. Kisāgotamī Vatthu.-The Buddha praises Kisāgotamī (1) in presence of Sakka. DhA.iv.156f.
