• Surasena

1. Surasena. One of the sixteen Mahājanapadā (q.v.). It is mentioned with Maccha, and was located in the south of the Kuru country. Its capital was Mathurā. It is famous in the Epics and the Purānas because of its connection with Krsna, and the Yādavas.

2. Surasena. A city in the time of Siddhattha Buddha, where the Bodhisatta was born as the brahmin Mangala. BuA.187.
