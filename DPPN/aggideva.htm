• Aggideva

1. Aggideva.-Fifth son of Devagabbhā and Upasāgara (J.iv.81f.; PvA.93 and 111), and one of the ten brothers who were famed as the Andhavenhudāsaputtā.

2. Aggideva.-See Aggibhagavā.

3. Aggideva.-A Cakka-vatti who lived eleven kalpas ago; a previous birth of Pāpanivāriya Thera. Ap.i.213.
