• Pādapūjaka Thera

1. Pādapūjaka Thera. An arahant. In the past he scattered seven jasmine flowers on the feet of Siddhattha Buddha. Five kappas ago he was king thirteen times under the name of Samantagandha (Samantabhadda) (Ap.i.141f). He is probably identical with Uttiya Thera. ThagA.i.125.

2. Pādapūjaka Thera. An arahant. Ninety one kappas ago he was a kinnara who, seeing Vipassī Buddha, gave him sandalwood, tagara and other perfumes. Ap.i.246.
