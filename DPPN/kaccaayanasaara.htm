• Kaccāyanasāra

A resume of the Kaccāyana-vyākarana by Mahāyasa (But see Bode, 36, n.3, and Gv.74), probably of the fourteenth century. It contains quotations from such treatises as the Bālavatāra, Rūpasiddhi, Cūlanirutti and Sambandhacintā. One tika on it was written by Mahāyasa himself, and another, called the Sammohavināsinī, by Saddhammavilāsa. Bode, 37.
