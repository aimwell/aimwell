• Vanappavesanakhanda

1. Vanappavesanakhanda. The third section of the Bhūridatta Jātaka, which deals with the return of Alambāyana and Somadatta from the Nāga world and their entering the forest for their livelihood. J.vi.170-7.

2. Vanappavesanakhanda. A section of the Vessantara Jātaka, dealing with the journey of Vessantara and his family from Jetuttara to Vankapabbata and their life in the hermitage prior to the arrival of Jūjaka. J.vi.513-21.
