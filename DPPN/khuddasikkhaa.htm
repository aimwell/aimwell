• Khuddasikkhā

A Compendium of the Vinaya, composed by Dhammasiri and ascribed to a period before Buddhaghosa (J.P.T.S., 1883, pp.86f). To this Compendium there exists a Sinhalese paraphrase of the eleventh century, and based on the Compendium is one Porāna-tīkā by Revata and another by Sangharakkhita. For details see P.L.C., 77; Bode, op. cit., 24; see also Sās.69; Svd. 1208f; Gv.62, 70; SadS.64.
