• Itthā Sutta

1. Itthā Sutta.-Preached to Anāthapindika on five things in the world which are very desirable but are difficult to attain — longevity, beauty, happiness, fame, happy rebirth — and on the means of obtaining them (A.iii.47-9).

2. Itthā Sutta.-The ten desirable things in the world, the obstacles to their attainment and the methods of procuring them (A.v.135f).
