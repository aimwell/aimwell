• Duvera or Anāthapindika Sutta

Anāthapindika visits the Buddha, who tells him of the five guilty fears — that begotten

  • by killing,
  • by stealing,
  • by wrong indulgence of sensual lust,
  • by evil speaking, and
  • by the drinking of intoxicants

- freedom from which, together with possession of the Noble Insight, enables a man to say that he is destined for Enlightenment. S.v.387f.
