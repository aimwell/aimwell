• Rūpavatī

1. Rūpavatī. Daughter of Vijayabāhu I. and Tilokasundarī. She had four sisters, and a brother called Vikkamabāhu (Cv.lix.31). She died young and unmarried (Cv.lix.45).

2. Rūpavatī. Queen of Parakkamabāhu I. She was a descendant of King Kittisirimegha. She is mentioned as having erected a “golden thūpa in Pulatthipura. Cv.lxxiii.137, 142ff. The thūpa has been identified with the modern. Pabulu Vehera (Arch. Survey of Ceylon, vi.1014, p. 6).
