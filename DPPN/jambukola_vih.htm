• Jambukola-vihāra

1. Jambukola-vihāra.-See Jambukola.

2. Jambukola-vihāra.-Another vihāra, with its celebrated rock-temple called the Jambukolalena, in the centre of Ceylon, twenty-six miles north of modern Matale. It was restored by Vijayabāhu I (Cv.lx.60) and rebuilt by Kittinissanka, who placed in it seventy-three golden statues of the Buddha. Cv.lxxx.23; see also Cv. Trs.ii.128, n.3.
