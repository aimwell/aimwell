• Sirī

1. Sirī. The goddess of Luck; she was the daughter of Dhatarattha (J.iii.257). For a story about her see the Sirikālakanni Jātaka. She is identified with Uppalavannā (J.iii.264).

2. Sirī. One of the four daughters of Sakka (J.v.392). See the Sudhābhojana Jātaka.

3. Sirī. See the Siri Jātaka. There Siri is personified as Luck. See also DA.i.97; MU. 191; cf. Lakkhī.
