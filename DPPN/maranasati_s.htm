• Maranasati Sutta

1. Maranasati Sutta. The Buddha tells the monks at Nādika that mindfulness of death, if properly cultivated, leads to great advantages. Various monks thereupon claim that they practise such mindfulness, but the Buddha tells them their practice is not sufficiently diligent and instructs them how to improve it. A.iv.317f.

2. Maranasati Sutta. Preached at Nādika. A monk must ever remember that death may overtake him at any instant. He must, therefore, examine himself day and night and put away any evil states that may remain in him. A.iv.320f.
