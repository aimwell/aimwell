• Agārava Sutta

1. Agārava Sutta.-On the five qualities that make a monk rebellious and unamenable to discipline. A.iii.7f.

2. Agārava Sutta.-A monk who is rebellious will never lead the higher life nor attain in the end to peace of mind. A.iii.14f.

3. Agārava Sutta.-The rebellious monk will never live according to the dhamma, nor thereby ultimately win insight. A.iii.15f.
