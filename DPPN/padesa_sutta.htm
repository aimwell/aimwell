• Padesa Sutta

1. Padesa Sutta

A conversation between Sāriputta, Anuruddha and Moggallāna in the Ketakivana in Sāketa, regarding the meaning of the word “sekha.” A sekha is one who has only partially cultivated the four satipatthānas. S.v.174f.

2. Padesa Sutta

Whoever cultivates psychic power, partially, can only do so by cultivating and developing the four bases of psychic power (iddhi-pādā). S.v.255.
