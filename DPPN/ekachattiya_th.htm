• Ekachattiya Thera

An arahant. In the time of Atthadassī he was an ascetic with a large following, living on the banks of the Candabhāgā. He visited the Buddha with his followers and held a white parasol over the Buddha’s head.

Seventy-seven kappas ago he lived in the deva-world as king of the devas. He was king of men one thousand times (Ap.ii.367ff).
