• Subhaddā

1. Subhaddā. Aggasāvikā of Revata Buddha. J.i.35; Bu.vi.22.

2. Subhaddā. One of the chief women lay supporters of Sujāta Buddha. Bu.xiii.30.

3. Subhaddā. Wife of Tissa Buddha before his renunciation. Bu.xviii.18.

4. Subhaddā. See Mahā-Subhaddā and Cūla-Subhaddā.

5. Subhaddā. Daughter of the Madda king and wife of the king of Benares. See the Chaddanta Jātaka.

6. Subhaddā. Wife of Mahāsudassana. See Mahāsubhaddā.

7. Subhaddā. A celestial musician or a musical instrument. VvA.94, 211.

8. Subhaddā. One of the five daughters of Vijayabāhu I. and Tilokasundarī. She married Vīrabāhu. Cv.lix.31, 43.
