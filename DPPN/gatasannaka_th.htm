• Gatasaññaka Thera

1. Gatasaññaka Thera.-An arahant. In the time of Tissa Buddha he joined the Order at the age of seven, and threw up into the air seven nangaliki-flowers as offering to the Buddha. Eight kappas ago he became king, three times, under the name of Aggisikha. Ap.i.127.

2. Gatasaññaka Thera.-An arahant. He saw the Buddha Siddhattha going through the air, though only his robe was visible to him. The sight filled him with joy and he concentrated his mind thereon. Ap.i.253f.
