• Ādiya Sutta

Preached at Jetavana to Anāthapindika on the five uses (ādiyā) of possessions legitimately obtained: one enjoys them oneself; entertains with them one’s friends and relations; uses them in times of need; employs them in the discharge of one’s duties to the king, to religion, to one’s relations, both living and dead; and in doing good deeds which will bring happiness in future lives. A.iii.45f.
