• Migācira

1. Migācira. A park in Benares (J.v.68, 476, 506). This seems to have been an old name for Isipatana, for it was the scene of Sikhī Buddha’s first sermon, (BuA. 205) and all Buddhas preach their first sermon in the same place. See Buddha.

2. Migācira. A park near Indapatta, which once belonged to Dhanañjaya Korabba (J.vi.256). It existed even in the time of the Buddha, for Ratthapāla Thera is mentioned as having stayed there. M.ii.65; MA.ii.725, 730; but ThagA.ii.34 calls it Migājina.
