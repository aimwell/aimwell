• Atthavyākhyāna

1. Atthavyākhyāna.-By Cūlabuddha Thera of Ceylon; a book on grammar or exegesis. Sas.34; Bode: Pali Lit. of Burma, 28.

2. Atthavyākhyāna.-By Culla-Vajira (of Ceylon). Gv.60.

3. Atthavyākhyāna.-By Culla-Vimalabuddhi; written, says the Gandhavamsa, independently, according to his own convictions. Gv.70.
