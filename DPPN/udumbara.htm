• Udumbara

1. Udumbara.-A thera of Makuva, author of a tīkā on the Petakopadesa. Gv.75, 65.

2. Udumbara.-A village. Revata went there from Kannakujja and stopped there before proceeding to Aggalapura and Sahajāti. Thither the Elders followed him to ask his opinion on the Vajjian heresy. Vin.ii.299.
