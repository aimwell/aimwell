• Pabhāvatī

1. Pabhāvatī. Mother of Sujāta Buddha. Bu.xiii.20; J.i.38.

2. Pabhāvatī. Mother of Sikkhī Buddha. Bu.xxi.15; J.i.41; D.ii.6; AA.i.436.

3. Pabhāvatī. Daughter of King Madda of Sāgala and wife of Kusa. For her story see the Kusa Jātaka. J.v.283ff.; Mtu.ii.441f. calls her Sudarsanā and her father Mahendraka.

4. Pabhāvatī. Daughter of Mānābharana (1) and sister of Parakkamabāhu 1. (Cv.lxii.3). She married Mānābharana (2), son of Sirivallabha. Ibid., lxiv.24.
