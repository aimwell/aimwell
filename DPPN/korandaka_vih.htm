• Korandaka Vihāra

A monastery in Ceylon. The Visuddhi Magga (p.91) contains the story of a young monk, nephew of the Elder of the Vihāra, who went to Rohana for study; he later returned to the Vihāra, and for three months was waited on by his parents, but he did not reveal his identity, fearing that his parents would prove an impediment to him.

This monastery was once the residence of an Elder named Mahā Sangharakkhita. v.l. Corakandaka. MT.606.
