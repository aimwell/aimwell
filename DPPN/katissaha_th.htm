• Katissaha Thera

1. Katissaha Thera.-An arahant. He was one of those who were staying with the Buddha at the Kūtāgārasālā in Vesāli. When the Licchavis began to visit the Buddha in large numbers, they left the monastery and retired to places of solitude, such as Gosingasālavana. A.v.133.

2. Katissaha.-One of the chief supporters of Dhammadassī Buddha. Bu.xvi.20.
