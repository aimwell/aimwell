• Kāmabhū

A monk, evidently held in high esteem by his colleagues. He is mentioned as staying in Kosambī, in Ghosita Park, and as asking Ānanda certain questions, recorded in the Kāmabhū Sutta (1) (S.iv.165). Two other suttas bearing the same name record visits paid to Kāmabhū by Cittagahapati at Ambātakavana in Macchikāsanda. S.iv.291, 293.
