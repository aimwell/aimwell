• Mahārattha

1. Mahārattha. A country where Mahādhammarakkhita went after the Third Council. It is generally identified with the country of the Marāthī at the source of the Godāvarī. Mhv.xii.5, 37; Dpv.viii.8; Sp.i.64,67.

2. Mahārattha. A district in Ceylon, near Kālavāpi, to the east. It held the village of Pillavatthi. Cv.lxxii.141, 163, 190, 199; also Cv.Trs.i.333, n.3.
