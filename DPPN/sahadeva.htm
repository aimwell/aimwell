• Sahadeva

1. Sahadeva Thera. An arahant. He accompanied the Thera Majjhima to the region of Himavā. Sp.i.68; Dpv.viii.10; MT.317.

2. Sahadeva. Son of the Pandu King. He was the youngest of five brothers, all husbands of Kanhā, the others being Ajjuna, Nakula, Bhīmasena and Yudhitthira. J.v.424, 426.
