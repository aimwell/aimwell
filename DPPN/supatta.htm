• Supatta

1. Supatta. The Bodhisatta born as a crow; see the Supatta Jātaka.

2. Supatta. A king of the vultures and son of the Bodhisatta. See the Gijjha Jātaka (No. 427).

3. Supatta. One of the five horses of King Kappina. Only the king rode Supatta, while messengers were allowed to ride the others. DhA.ii.117.
