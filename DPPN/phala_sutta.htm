• Phala Sutta

1. Phala Sutta

The cultivation of the five indriyas leads to one of two results: either realization in this life, or the state of anāgāmī. S.v.236.

2. Phala Sutta

Same as (1), only substituting the four iddhipādas for the five indriyas. S.v.285f.

3. Phala Sutta

On seven fruits to be obtained from the cultivation of the four iddhipādas. S.v.285.

4. Phala Sutta

Four conditions which, if cultivated, lead to the Four Fruits of the Path. S.v.410f.
