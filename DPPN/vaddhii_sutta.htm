• Vaddhī Sutta

1. Vaddhī Sutta. An Ariyan woman disciple increases in five things: faith, virtue, learning, generosity and wisdom. S.iv.250= A.iii.80.

2. Vaddhī Sutta. The same as Sutta (1), but as applied to a man. A.iii.80.

3. Vaddhī Sutta. The Ariyan (disciple grows in ten ways: in lands and fields, wealth and possessions, wife and family, servitors and retinue, beasts of burden, faith, virtue, learning, generosity and wisdom. A.v.137.
