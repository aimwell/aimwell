• Gāmanī

1. Gāmanī.-A Tamil general and his stronghold, subdued by Dutthagāmanī. Mhv.xxv.13.

2. Gāmanī.-A prince, youngest of one hundred brothers. His story is given in the Samvara Jātaka. J.iv.130ff; see also J.i.136f.

3. Gāmanī.-One of the six brothers of Bhaddakaccānā; he remained behind when the others left for Ceylon. MT.275.

4. Gāmanī.-See also Dīgha-gāmanī, Duttha-gāmanī and Ānanda -gāmani.
