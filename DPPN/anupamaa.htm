• Anupamā

1. Anupamā.-Daughter of the banker Anupama (9), of the village of the same name. She gave a meal of milk-rice to Anomadassī Buddha just before his Enlightenment. BuA.142.

2. Anupamā.-Daughter of the banker Anupama (10), of the village Anoma. She gave a meal of milk-rice to Sumana Buddha just prior to his Enlightenment. BuA.125.

3. Anupamā.-See Māgandiyā.
