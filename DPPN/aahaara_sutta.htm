• Āhāra Sutta

1. Āhāra Sutta.-Preached at Jetavana on the four sustenances (āhārā) that maintain beings by bringing them to birth and keeping them after birth; also the cause of these sustenances and the method of their cessation. S.ii.11-12.

2. Āhāra Sutta.-A group of suttas dealing with the food of the nīvaranas and of the bojjhangas, and with the condition that follows on the absence of their food. S.v.102-7.
