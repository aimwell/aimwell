• Cūlābhayasumana

A Thera who lived in the Mahāvihāra in the time of King Bhātiya.

He was an authority on the Vinaya, and a story is related in the Samantapāsādikā (Sp.ii.305f) of how he was consulted by a monk who having picked up in a crowd a robe that fell from the shoulders of another monk concealed it, but later wished to return it to the owner.
