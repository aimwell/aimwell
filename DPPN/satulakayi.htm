• Satulakāyī

The wife of Jotika.

She came to him from Uttarakuru and brought with her a single pint pot of rice and three burning glasses (crystals). Whenever a meal was desired, the pot was placed on the crystals and the crystals blazed up. The food so prepared was never exhausted. When Jotika joined the Order, the divinities took Satulakāyī back to Uttarakuru. DhA.iv.209, 223.
