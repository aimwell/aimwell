• Sīsupacalā Therī

One of the sisters of Sāriputta.

Māra once tempted her with thoughts of the pleasures of the Kāmaloka (sensuous world), but she sent him away discomfited (Thig.vss.190-203; ThigA.168f.; S.i.133f).

Her story resembles that of Cālā. She had a son of the same name as herself. ThagA.i.110.
