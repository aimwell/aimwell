• Sanditthika Sutta

1. Sanditthika Sutta. A conversation between the Buddha and Moliya Sīvaka on how the dhamma is for this life. A.iii.356.

2. Sanditthika Sutta. The same as (1), but the conversation is with a brahmin. A.iii.357.

3. Sanditthika Sutta. Ānanda explains to Udāyī (Kāludāyī) how the Buddha’s teaching is to be seen for oneself in this life. A.iv.453.

4. Sanditthika Sutta. The same as (3), on how nibbāna can be realized in this life. A.iv.453.
