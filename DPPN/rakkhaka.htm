• Rakkhaka

1. Rakkhaka. A general of Vikkamabāhu II., he was captured by Vīradeva. Cv.lxi.42.

2. Rakkhaka Ilanga. A general of Dappula IV. He built a dwelling house near the Thūpārāma, which was named after the king. Cv.liii.11.

3. Rakkhaka Sankhanāyaka. An officer of Parakkamabāhu I., who stationed him at Hillapattakakhanda. Cv.lxxii.41.
