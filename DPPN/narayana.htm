• Nārāyana

1. Nārāyana

The name of a god (Visnu). E.g., Cv.xlvii.25.

2. Nārāyana

A general of Parakkamabāhu I., in charge of Anurādhapura. He rose in rebellion against the king and was slain in battle. Cv.lxxii.65.

3. Nārāyana

A Damila chief, one of the three Vīrapparāyaras. He was an ally of Lankāpura, general of Parakkamabāhu I. Cv.lxxvii.6.
