• Pandaka

1. Pandaka

A monk, friend of Kapila, who lived in a village near Kosambī. He was found guilty of having taken what did not belong to him and also of unchastity. Vin.iii.67.

2. Pandaka

A Yakkha of the Himālaya region. He, his wife Hāritā, and his five hundred sons, became sotāpannas when Majjhantika preached to Aravāla. Mhv.xii.21.
