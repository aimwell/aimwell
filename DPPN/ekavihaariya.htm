• Ekavihāriya

1. Ekavihāriya Thera.-An arahant. In the time of Kassapa Buddha he entered the Order and dwelt in solitude. In this life, too, he was fond of dwelling alone. Ap.ii.390f.

2. Ekavihāriya.-A monk greatly famed for his love of solitude. When they told the Buddha of him, the Buddha sang his praises in the midst of the assembly. DhA.iii.471f.

3. Ekavihāriya.-See Tissakumāra, brother of Asoka.
