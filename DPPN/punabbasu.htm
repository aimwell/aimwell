• Punabbasu

1. Punabbasu. A young Yakkha (S.i.210). He became a sotāpanna (SA.i.239). See Punabbasumātā.

2. Punabbasu. One of the Chabbaggiyā. His followers were called Panabbasukā, and, together with the followers of Assaji, they were called Assaji Punabbasukā (q.v.).

Punabbasu Sutta. Records an incident relating to Punabbasumātā. (q.v.). S.i.210.
