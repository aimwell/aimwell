• Saccasankhepa

A short treatise of five chapters on Abhidhamma topics.

It was written by Culladhammapāla. (Gv.60, 71, 75; SaS.89; Svd.1220).

The Saddhamma-Sangaha (p. 64) attributes it to Dhammapala’s teacher, Ānanda. Vācissara and Sumangala wrote tīkās on it, the first being older than the second. P.L.C.203f.
