• Sahassarāja

1. Sahassarāja. One hundred and sixty five kappas ago, there were three kings of this name, previous births of Udakapūjaka (Kutivihāriya) Thera. Ap.i.143; ThagA.i.129.

2. Sahassarāja. Eleven kappas ago there were eight kings of this name, previous births of Dhammacakkika Thera. Ap.i.90.
