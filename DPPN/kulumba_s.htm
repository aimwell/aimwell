• Kulumba Sutta

A discourse, evidently well known, but not included in the Three Rescensions (tisso sañgīti) (Sp.iv.742, 743).

The Atthasālinī (DhSA.91) gives an extract from it and refers “to an infanticide of Kulumba.” The quotation is to show that a bodily action may arise also in the mind-door.
