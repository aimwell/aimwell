• Atthinukhopariyāya Sutta

Is there a method by following which a monk could affirm that he has won insight? “Yes,” answers the Buddha; a monk beholding an object or hearing a sound, etc., recognises it with the eye of wisdom and of reason, whether it produces in him lust, etc., or not. This method leads to insight apart from belief, hearsay, etc. S.i.138.
