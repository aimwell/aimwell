• Kundi, Kundiya

1. Kundi, Kundiya.-A village of the Kurus. Near the village was a forest where lived Anganika-Bhāradvāja. Close by was the Uggārāma. v.l. Kundikola. ThagA.i.339.

2. Kundi.-A village of the Koliyans, near which was the Kundadhāna-vana (q.v.). It was the birthplace of Kundanagariya (Potthapāda) Thera. Near it was the Sānavāsipabbata where once Ānanda stayed. PvA.178.
