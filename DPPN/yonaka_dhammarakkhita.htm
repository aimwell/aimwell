• Yonaka Dhammarakkhita

1. Yonaka Dhammarakkhita Thera. He was sent to Aparantaka at the conclusion of Moggaliputtapissa’s Council (Mhv.xii.4).' There he preached the Aggikkhandhopama Sutta and converted thirty seven thousand beings. Mhv.xii.34f.; Dpv.viii.7; Sp.i.67.

2. Yonaka Dhammarakkhita. Teacher of Punabbasukutumbikaputta Tīssa (q.v.).
