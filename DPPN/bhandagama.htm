• Bhandagāma

A Vajjian village between Vesāli and Hatthigāma and near the former.

The Buddha visited it during his last tour, and while there he talked to the monks on four conditions, which lead to Nibbāna:

  • righteousness,
  • earnest thought,
  • wisdom, and
  • freedom.

D.ii.123; A.ii.1ff.
