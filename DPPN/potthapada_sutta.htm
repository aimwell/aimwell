• Potthapāda Sutta

A discussion between the Buddha and the Paribbājaka Potthapāda, held at the Mallikārāma (D.i.178 203). It deals with the question of trances and of the soul, and also with the infinity and eternalism of the world.

This sutta is significant as containing a list of the topics which Paribbājakas appear to have discussed at their meetings.
