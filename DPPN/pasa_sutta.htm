• Pāsa Sutta

1. Pāsa Sutta

Preached at the Migadāya in Isipatana. The Buddha tells the monks that he realized supreme emancipation by means of yonisomanasikāra, and asserts that he is free from Māra’s snares. S.i.105.

2. Pāsa Sutta

Preached at Isipatana. The Buddha declares that he is free from all snares, both celestial and human, and admonishes the monks to wander about for the good of the many. He himself was going to Senānigama in Uruvelā to preach. S.i.106.
