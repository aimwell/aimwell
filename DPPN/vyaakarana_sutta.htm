• Vyākarana Sutta

1. Vyākarana Sutta. Five qualities, including knowledge of the four kinds of analysis, which enable a monk to attain his aim. A.iii.110.

2. Vyākarana Sutta. Preached by Mahā Moggallāna, on ten qualities which should be abandoned in order to achieve one’s purpose in the sāsana. A.v.155f.
