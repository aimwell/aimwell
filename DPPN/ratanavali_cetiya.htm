• Ratanāvali cetiya

1. Ratanāvali cetiya. Another name for the Mahā Thūpa (q.v.).

2. Ratanāvali cetiya. A thūpa in Pulatthipura. It was probably built by Parakkamabāhu I. and restored and crowned with a golden finial by Kittinissanka. Cv.lxxx.20; also Cv.Trs.ii.128, n.1; 107, n.3.

3. Ratanāvali cetiya. A thūpa erected in Khīragāma by Parakkamabāhu I. on the site of his mother’s pyre. Cv.lxxix.71; Cv.Trs.ii.122, n.7.
