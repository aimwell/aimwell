• Kaccāyanabheda

Also called Kaccāyanabhedadīpikā. A treatise on Kaccāyana’s grammar, written by Mahāyasa of Thaton, probably about the fourteenth century. A tika on it, called the Sāratthavikāsini, was written by Ariyālankāra. Svd.1250; Bode, op. cit., 36f, but see Gv.74, where the author is called Dhammānanda.
