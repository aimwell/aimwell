• Kāladevala

1. Kāladevala.-A sage; see Asita-Devala (2).

2. Kāladevala.-An ascetic, friend and counsellor of Suddhodana (J.i.54, 67, 88; vi.479). See Asita (1).

He was a frequent visitor at the palace, and the Buddha’s mother always took the precepts from him until she conceived the Buddha. MA.ii.922.
