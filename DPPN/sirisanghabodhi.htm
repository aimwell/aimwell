• Sirisanghabodhi

1. Sirisanghabodhi. See Sanghabodhi.

2. Sirisanghabodhi. The surname of Aggabodhi III., (Cv.xliv.83) of Aggabodhi IV., (Cv.xlvi.1) and Vijayabāhu I. (Cv.lix.10).

1. Sirisanghabodhi-vihāra. A monastery, built by Meghavannābhayā, to the south of Issarasamana-vihāra and on the spot where Sanghabodhi’s (q.v.) body was cremated. MT. 671.

2. Sirisanghabodhi-vihāra. A parivena built by Aggabodhi I. (Cv.xlii.11). Mānavamma built the Siripāsāda there. Cv.xlvii.64.
