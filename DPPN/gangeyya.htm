• Gangeyya

1. Gangeyya.-Adjective formed from Ganga (J.ii.151). The description “Gangeyya nāgaraja” in J.iii.362 probably means a nāga king “dwelling in Ganga” and not “named Gangeyya.”

2. Gangeyya.-One of the ten families of elephants. Each elephant had the strength of one hundred men. MA.i.262; AA.ii.822; BuA.37.
