• Anatta Sutta

1. Anatta Sutta.-Preached to Rādha at Sāvatthi in answer to his question “What is not-self ?” S.iii.196.

2 and 3. Anatta Suttas.-The occasion is the same. That which is without a self must be put away. S.iii.199 and 201.

4. Anatta Sutta.-The idea of “not-self,” when cultivated, conduces to great profit. S.v.133.
