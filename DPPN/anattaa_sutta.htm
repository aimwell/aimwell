• Anattā Sutta

1. Anattā Sutta.-All the khandhas are without the self. The Ariyan disciple feels revulsion towards them realising that, for him, there is no hereafter (S.iii.21).

2. Anattā Sutta.-Same as above (S.iii.77).

3. Anattā Sutta.-All objects of the senses (sights, sounds, etc.), both external (bāhira) and personal (ajjhatta), are void of a self. S.iv.2, 4, 6.

4. Anattā Sutta.-Everything is void of self. S.iv.28.
