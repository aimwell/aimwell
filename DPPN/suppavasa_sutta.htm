• Suppavāsā Sutta

The Buddha visits Suppavāsā in Sajjanela and is entertained by her.

He preaches to her that an Ariyan upāsikā, who gives food, gives four things to the receiver of the food

  1. life,
  2. beauty,
  3. happiness and
  4. strength

and is sure of happiness in later lives. A.ii.62f.
