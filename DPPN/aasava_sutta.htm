• Āsava Sutta

1. Āsava Sutta.-On the six qualities which make a monk worthy of honour and offerings, due to destruction of the āsavas, and also on the methods which lead to such destruction. A.iii.387-94.

2. Āsava Sutta.-Ten things that conduce to the destruction of the āsavas: the eight factors of the Noble Eightfold Path, in addition to sammāñāna and sammāvimutti. A.v.237.
