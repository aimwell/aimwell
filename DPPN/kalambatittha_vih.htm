• Kalambatittha-Vihāra

A vihāra in Ceylon. In it once lived fifty monks who agreed on the full-moon day of Āsālha not to talk to each other until they became arahants. Their goal was reached within a month (DA.i.190; SA.iii.155; SnA.i.57; VibhA.353; MA.i.209f).

This vihāra is very probably identical with Galambatittha (q.v.).
