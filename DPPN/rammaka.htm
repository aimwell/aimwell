• Rammaka

1. Rammaka. A brahmin whose hermitage was in Sāvatthi, near the Pubbakotthaka. It was a great resort of the monks, and there the Buddha preached the Ariyapariyesana Sutta. M.i.160.

2. Rammaka. A city where, sixty thousand kappas ago, Mahā Kassapa reigned as King Ubbiddha. Ap.i.34.

3. Rammaka. See Ramma (4).
