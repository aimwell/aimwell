• Vāsabhagāmī, Vāsabhagāmika

A Thera, pupil of Anuruddha Thera.

He was one of the four Pācīnaka monks appointed to the committee (ubbāhikā) which considered the Ten Points raised by the Vajjiputtakā.

His fellow pupil was Sumana, and they had both seen the Buddha. Vin.ii.305; Dpv.iv.51; v. 22, 24; Sp.i.35; Mhv.iv.48, 58.
