• Maggasaññaka Thera

An arahant. In the time of Padumuttara Buddha he was a devaputta in Himavā, and, coming across some monks who had lost their way in the forest, he entertained them and set them on the right road. One hundred and five kappas ago he was king twelve times under the name of Sacakkhu (Ap.i.152f). He is probably identical with Ekadhammasavaniya. ThagA.i.151f.
