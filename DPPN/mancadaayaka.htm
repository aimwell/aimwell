• Mañcadāyaka

1. Mañcadāyaka Thera. An arahant. In the time of Siddhattha Buddha he was a Candāla, and made a lintel which he offered to the Order. He was fifty times king of the gods and eighty times king of men. Ap.ii.377f.

2. Mañcadāyaka. An arahant Thera. Ninety one kappas ago he gave a bed to Vipassī Buddha (Ap.ii.455). He is probably identical with Sāmaññakāni Thera (ThagA.i.99). v.l. Pecchadāyaka.
