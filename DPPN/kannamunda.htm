• Kannamunda, Kannamundaka

One of the seven great lakes of Himavā. (A.iv.101; DA.i.164; J.v.415; AA.ii.759; MA.ii.692, etc.). Its waters never grew hot (SnA.ii.407). Once a mango from a tree growing on the banks of this lake came into the possession of King Dadhivāhana, and from its stone grew a tree which bore very sweet fruit (J.ii.104).
