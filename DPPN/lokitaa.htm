• Lokitā

1. Lokitā. A Singhalese princess, sister of Devalā. She married the son of her paternal aunt, Kassapa (later Vikkamabāhu I.), and had two sons, Moggallāna and Loka. Cv.lvii.27f.

2. Lokitā. A princess, daughter of Bodhi and Buddhā. She married Moggallāna and had by him four children: Kitti (afterwards Vijayabāhu I.), Mittā, Mahinda and Rakkhita. Cv.lvii.41.
