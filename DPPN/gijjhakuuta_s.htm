• Gijjhakūta Sutta

(commonly called Abhaya Sutta)

Contains an account of the visit paid by Abhayarājakumāra to the Buddha at Gijjhakūta.

Abhaya questions the Buddha on the views of Pūrana Kassapa.

The Buddha declares these to be wrong, and instructs Abhaya in the nīvaranas and the bojjhangas. S.v.126f.
