• Gāmendavālamahā-vihāra

A monastery in Rohana. It was the residence of Cūlapindapātikatissa and of Milakkhatissa (AA.i.21).

It was evidently within easy reach of Cittalapabbata and Kājaragāma (AA.i.22).

Once Maliyadeva Thera recited the Cha Chakka Sutta at this vihāra, and at the end of the recital sixty monks became arahants. MA.ii.1024.
