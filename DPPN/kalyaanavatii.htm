• Kalyānavatī

The first queen-consort of Kittinissanka.

After the death of Sāhasamalla she carried on the government of Ceylon for six months (according to some six years, 1202-1208 A.C.) with the help of her general, Ayasmanta.

She built a vihāra called the Kalyānavatī-vihāra in the village of Pannasālaka. Cv.lxxx.34ff; also Cv.Trs.ii.130, n.3.
