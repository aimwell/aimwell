• Candaka

1. Candaka.-Another name for Canda (Candakumāra).

2. Candaka.-The palace of King Sivi. J.iv.411.

3. Candaka.-The palace of Angati, king of Videha (J.vi.229, 230, 231). v.l. Canda (J.vi.242).

4. Candaka.-One of the palaces to be occupied by the future Buddha Metteyya. Anāgatavamsa, vs.46.
