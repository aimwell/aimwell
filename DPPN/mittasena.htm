• Mittasena

1. Mittasena Thera. One of the eminent monks who took a leading part in the Foundation Ceremony of the Mahā Thūpa. Dpv.xix.8; MT. 525, 527.

2. Mittasena. A thief (? vīhicora) who succeeded Sotthisena and ruled for one year (432 33 A.C.) at Anurādhapura, after the murder of Sotthisena by Sanghā. He restored some of the cetiyas an(d was slain by the Damila Pandu. Cv.xxxviii.5ff.
