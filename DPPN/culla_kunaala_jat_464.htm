• Culla-Kunāla Jātaka (No.464)

For this story reference is made to the Kunāla Jātaka.

The opening words quoted occur at the end of several stories, related by Kunāla to illustrate the fickleness and wickedness of women.

Culla-Kunāla is probably the name given to the last of these stories, which tells of Pingiyānī, wife of Brahmadatta. J.iv.144.
