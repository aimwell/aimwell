• Dhammahadayavibhanga Sutta

In the Sutta-Sangaha, a portion of the Dhammahadayavibhanga of the Vibhangappakarana (Vibh.422f ) — dealing with birth in the Kāmāvacara worlds, the good deeds that lead to birth therein, and the span of life in each world — has been taken as a separate sutta (No.14) and called by this name.
