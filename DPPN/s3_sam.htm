It was in the Malaya country, and Vattagāmanī Abhaya lived there during a part of his exile in the house of Tanasīva.

  • Samajīvī Sutta. The Buddha visits the house of Nakulapitā, while staying in the Bhesakalāvana on Sumsumāragiri. That, says the Buddha, is possible if they are matched in faith, virtue, generosity and wisdom. A.ii.61f.
