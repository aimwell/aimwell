• Devarāja

1. Devarāja.-A general of Parakkamabāhu I. He held the office of Kesadhātu and lived in Pañcayojana. He won a great victory at Gimhatittha. Cv.lxxv.21.

2. Devarāja.-A vihāra in Rohana, the residence of Piyadassī, author of the Padasādhana. Devarāja formed part of the Rambhā-vihāra. P.L.C.205.
