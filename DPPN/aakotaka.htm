• Ākotaka

A deva who visited the Buddha at Veluvana accompanied by Asama, Sahali, Ninka, Vetambarī and Mānava-Gāmiya.

Ākotaka spoke before the Buddha in praise of various teachers of other schools:

  • Pakuddha-Kātiyāna (sic.),

  • Nigantha

  • Makkhali

  • Pūrana.

Vetambari made rejoinder to Ākotaka, speaking disparagingly of the teachers he had mentioned. S.i.65.
