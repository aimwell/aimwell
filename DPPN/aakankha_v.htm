• Ākankha Vagga

The eighth chapter of the Dasaka Nipāta of the Anguttara Nikāya.

It consists of ten suttas on such subjects as the “thornless” life, the obstacles to desired things, Migasālā’s questions on the future life of individuals, the likeness of a bad monk to a crow, the qualities of the Niganthas, etc. A.v.131-51.
