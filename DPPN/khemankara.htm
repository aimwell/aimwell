• Khemankara

1. Khemankara Thera.-The constant attendant of Sikhī Buddha. v.l. Khemankura. (D.ii.6; Bu.xxi.20; J.i.41).

2. Khemankara.-The king of Khema or Khemavatī, where the Buddha Kakusandha was born. v.l. Khemākara. His purohita was Aggidatta, Kakusandha’s father (Bu.xxii.13; BuA.209; Dvy.242). He is sometimes also called Khema (MT.351; D.ii.7).
