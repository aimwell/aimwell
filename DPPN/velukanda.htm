• Velukanda, Velukanta

A city in Avanti, birthplace of Nanda Kumāputta (ThagA.i.100).

Moggallāna and Sāriputta visited the place in the course of a journey in Dakkhināgiri and were entertained by Nandamātā (A.iv.62f). See Velukantakī.

Buddhaghosa says (AA.ii.717; SNA.i.370) that the city was so called because bamboos were thickly planted for protection round the walls and fortifications.
