• Kīlamangala

1. Kīlamangala.-A district in South India which Lankāpura subdued and gave over to Mālava (Cv.lxxvi.209-11). Its chieftain (nādālvāra) was Kulasekhara’s ally (Cv.lxxvii.80). It was one of the two divisions of Mangala, the other being called Melamangala.

2. Kīlamangala.-A Damila chieftain, ally of Kulasekhara. Cv.lxxvii.80.
