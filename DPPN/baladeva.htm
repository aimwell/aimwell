• Baladeva

The second of the sons of Devagabbhā, the brothers known as the Andhakavenhuputtā.

Baladeva killed Cānura and Mutthika. The latter, when dying, vowed vengeance and, having been born as a goblin in the Kālamattikā forest, assumed the form of a wrestler when Baladeva passed that way and killed and ate him. J.iv.81, 82, 88; PvA.11, 93.
