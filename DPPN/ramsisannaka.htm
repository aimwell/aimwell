• Ramsisaññaka

1. Ramsisaññaka Thera. An arahant. He was an ascetic in Himavā, and seeing Vipassī Buddha radiating light, be paid him homage. Ap.i.129.

2. Ramsisaññaka. An arahant Thera. Ninety two kappas ago he was an ascetic, and seeing Phussa Buddha in trance, was overjoyed at the sight. Ap.i.130.

3. Ramsisaññaka. An arahant Thera. Thirty thousand kappas ago he saw a Buddha seated on a rock suffusing all the place with his aura, and was gladdened by the sight. Fifty seven kappas ago he was a king named Sujāta. Ap.i.210.
