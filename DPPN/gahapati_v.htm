• Gahapati Vagga

1. Gahapati Vagga.-The sixth section of the Majjhima Nikāya. M.i.339-413.

2. Gahapati Vagga.-The third section of the Atthaka Nipāta of the Anguttara Nikāya. It contains ten suttas, including the Buddha’s praises of Ugga of Vesāli, Ugga of Hatthigāma, and Hatthaka Ālavaka. A.iv.208-35.

3. Gahapati Vagga.-The fifth chapter of the Nidāna Samyutta. S.ii.68-80.

4. Gahapati Vagga.-The thirteenth chapter of the Salāyatana Samyutta. It contains conversations between various eminent householders on the one side, and the Buddha and the monks on the other. S.iv.109-24.
