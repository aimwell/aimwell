• Mūsila

1. Mūsila (v.l. Musīla, Musila). Devadatta born as the chief musician of Ujjeni. For his story see the Guttila Jātaka.

2. Mūsila Thera. A monk. A conversation is recorded in the Samyutta Nikayā (S.ii.115f ) between him and Savittha, which is said to have taken place in Ghositārāma in Kosambī, regarding the paticcasamuppāda.
